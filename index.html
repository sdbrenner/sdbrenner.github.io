About Me

I am a PhD student studying Oceanography at the University of Washington. I am a member of the Applied Physics Lab APL), and affiliated with the Environmental Fluid Mechanics (EFM) group, and am co-supervised by Luc Rainville and Jim Thomson.

Research Interests

My PhD research focuses on upper-ocean mixing processes in the Arctic Ocean. Specifically, I am a member of the Stratified Dynamics of the Arctic (SODA) project team.
